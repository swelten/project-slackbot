# Acquisition Folder Structure

```
  - 01_Intake
    - 01_Lead_Forms
    - 02_NDA
    - 03_Scoping_Notes
    - 04_Budget_Approvals
  - 02_Sales_Process
    - 01_Discovery_Meetings
      - YYYYMMDD_Topic
        - Documents
        - Notes
    - 02_Proposals
      - Drafts
      - Final
    - 03_Contracts
      - Negotiation
      - Signed
  - 03_Operations_Enablement
    - 01_Data_Room
      - Customer Assets
      - Internal Research
    - 02_Estimations
    - 03_Pilots_Demos
  - 04_Communication
    - 01_Stakeholder_Updates
    - 02_Workshops_Webinars
    - 03_Marketing_Material
    - 99_Mails
  - 05_Research_Proposals
    - 01_Call_Documents
    - 02_Concepts
    - 03_Partner_Documents
    - 04_Submissions
```

- `01_Intake`: Lead capture & qualification.
- `02_Sales_Process`: Discovery meetings, proposals, contracts.
- `03_Operations_Enablement`: Data rooms, estimations, pilots/demos.
- `04_Communication`: Stakeholder updates, workshops/webinars, marketing assets.
- `05_Research_Proposals`: Call docs, concepts, partner packages, submissions.
