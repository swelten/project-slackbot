# Acquisition Folder Structure

```
  - 01_Admin_and_Contracts
    - NDA
    - Signed_Contracts
  - 02_Input_and_Data
    - Client_Files
    - Notes_and_Meetings
  - 03_Proposals_and_Concepts
    - Drafts
    - Final_Sent
  - 04_Communication
    - Mails_and_Updates
```

- `01_Admin_and_Contracts`: All legal and administrative paperwork; keep NDAs and signed agreements separate for quick reference.
- `02_Input_and_Data`: Source material from the client plus internal notes or meeting summaries that shape the opportunity.
- `03_Proposals_and_Concepts`: Working drafts, calculations, and final versions that were sent to the client.
- `04_Communication`: Outbound status updates, stakeholder emails, and other communication artifacts.
